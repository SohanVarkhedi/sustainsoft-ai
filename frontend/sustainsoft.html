How It Works

Intelligent analysis. Real results.

01
Runtime monitoring
Always On
A lightweight background agent collects CPU, RAM, I/O, and process data continuously — with less than 0.5% overhead. System-native APIs on Windows, Linux, and macOS ensure zero performance impact.
02
Score computation
Real-Time
Weighted composite scoring normalizes all metrics into a 0–100 sustainability rating. Each metric contributes proportionally to its environmental impact — CPU usage weighted heaviest at 40%.
03
ML prediction
Predictive AI
Trained on historical resource consumption trends, our regression model predicts when a software's requirements will outpace your hardware — giving you months of warning before forced upgrades.
04
Actionable insights
For Everyone
Developers get component-level inefficiency reports. Organizations get total fleet sustainability dashboards. Consumers get plain-English explanations of which apps are killing their devices.
